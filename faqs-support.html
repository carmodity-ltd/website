Frequently Asked Questions

1

Can I drive into the United States?

Yes. Our insurance provides coverage in the US & Canada.

2

Can I add multiple drivers to my booking?

Yes. We just have to receive a picture of all the drivers’ licenses upon or prior to delivery.

3

What happens if I want to return late?

Call us at 778-926-4466 & let us know. There is an 80$ surcharge for late returns if there is another booking right after yours. Otherwise we will charge you 5$/hr upto 12 hours, after which the day rate will apply.

4

How do I extend my booking?

Similar to late returns, let our concierge know how many hours extra you need and we can charge your credit card or send you a payment link if you want to pay yourself.

5

Where do I pick up the car?

We deliver cars anywhere in Vancouver, North Vancouver, Burnaby, New Westminster and Richmond for free. A $20 surcharge is added to deliveries in West Vancouver, Coquitlam, Delta, Surrey and Steveston. Deliveries to Abbotsford will cost an additional $40. Our concierge will meet you in person for the first booking. For subsequent trips, we will deliver the car at the address you specified.

6

Where do I drop off the car?

Let our concierge know of your exact drop off location and we will pick up the car from there. If pickup surcharges were paid, you will not be charged anything extra for drop off.

7

How are gas costs handled?

Day bookings: We deliver the car to you with a full tank and at your expense you must return it full or we fill it up for you and charge your credit card.

Hourly bookings: Gas is included in all hourly bookings.

8

How are tickets, tow-aways, and tolls handled?

When we are notified that a ticket has been issued, a car has been towed, or a toll is left unpaid, we will let you know immediately. Unless you decide to dispute it, you can pay it off yourself within 5 days, or we will charge your credit card for it and pay it off ourselves.

9

Can I rent a car for only 1 hour?

Unfortunately our minimum is 3 hrs except on the UBC Campus, for which our concierge can create a custom booking for you.

10

What does Insurance cover?

All our cars are covered for at least $1,000,000 in 3rd Party Liability and Collision Damage. In the case of any accident or damage on the car, found to be at your fault, you are responsible for upto $500 if you declined protection. If you bought protection you are not responsible for damage costs. Stay safe!

11

What do I do in case of an accident?

Get the license plate details of all vehicles involved in the accident as well as their drivers' name, phone number and driver license number. Then let concierge know and we will handle from there.
